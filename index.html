Dom's Vision

With 22 years in creative direction, I've dedicated my career to elevating visual storytelling. After shooting high-budget campaigns in London and serving as Senior Creative Manager for Amazon Fashion Studios and Amazon Visual Innovations, I founded Aleandri Creative to bridge the gap between exceptional talent and visionary brands.

Based outside Pittsburgh and serving NYC and Columbus, we're not just an agency—we're a creative nexus where talent meets opportunity, and concepts become compelling visual narratives.
